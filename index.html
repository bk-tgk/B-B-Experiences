Our Story

B&B Experiences was founded by a couple who went through the same challenge many families face — placing their parents into senior living homes. Every visit brought the same requests: “Can you bring me my favorite snacks?” “I wish we could go somewhere fun.” “I need help getting to my doctor appointment.” We realized these needs were incredibly common across senior communities. So we created B&B Experiences — a service designed specifically to bring convenience, fun, and reliable support directly to senior living communities.

Our Three Experiences

B&B Treats

High-quality vending machines placed directly inside senior living communities. Residents vote every month on which snacks they want stocked. Favorites often include: • Milano Cookies • Peanut M&M's • Cheez-It Crackers We handle everything end-to-end: installation, restocking, and snack selection. Currently accepting 10 new senior communities.

B&B Tours

Once-a-month fun outings for seniors. Examples in the Bay Area include: • Santa Cruz Boardwalk • Monterey Bay Aquarium • Local museums and scenic drives We provide transportation, meals, water, prizes, and souvenirs. Current waitlist: 2 months.

B&B Transportation

Safe, insured rides specifically designed for seniors. Appointments include: • Doctor visits • Dental appointments • Physical therapy Drivers are trained to assist seniors with care and patience. Current waitlist: 3 months.
