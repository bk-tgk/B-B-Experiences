The three needs that inspired B&B Experiences

When our own parents moved into senior living, the same requests came up over and over. Those experiences became the foundation for everything we built.

1

“Can you bring me my favorite snacks?”

Familiar favorites matter. Treats like Peanut M&M’s, Milano Cookies, and Cheez-It Crackers may seem small, but they create comfort, choice, and something to look forward to.

2

“I wish there was something fun to do.”

Many seniors crave fresh scenery, special outings, and simple adventures. A well-planned field trip can bring joy, conversation, and a welcome break from routine.

3

“How do I get to my appointment?”

Transportation can be one of the most stressful gaps for families and facilities. Dependable rides matter, especially when the destination is important and timing is critical.

Why senior living communities partner with B&B Experiences

After years of selling into senior living, the strongest value proposition is simple: help the community offer something residents and families can feel immediately, while making life easier for staff behind the scenes.

Enhances resident experience

Residents feel seen when experiences reflect their actual preferences. Monthly snack voting, enjoyable outings, and dependable rides create moments that feel personal rather than generic.

Supports family peace of mind

Families want to know their loved one has more than just care. They want comfort, enjoyment, and practical help. B&B helps communities deliver that added layer of reassurance.

Reduces operational burden

Communities are busy. We remove the need for your team to source snacks, coordinate outings, or solve transportation requests one by one. We manage the process for you.

Adds a differentiating amenity

B&B Treats, Tours, and Transportation can strengthen the everyday experience your community offers, giving you another meaningful point of distinction for both current and prospective families.

Our three experience pillars

Each pillar is designed to be thoughtful, polished, and easy for communities to say yes to.

B&B Treats

A modern vending experience residents actually enjoy

B&B Treats brings intuitive, high-quality vending machines into senior living communities and turns them into something more personal, interactive, and fun.

  • Residents vote every month on the treats they want to see stocked
  • Popular favorites can include Peanut M&M’s, Milano Cookies, Cheez-It Crackers, trail mix, and more
  • We handle machine placement, snack sourcing, restocking, and ongoing management
  • Communities get a polished amenity without having to manage the details internally
Now accepting 10 communities on a first come, first served basis
B&B Tours

  • We coordinate transportation, food, water, prizes, and souvenirs

About B&B Experiences

We are a couple who built B&B Experiences after going through the very real experience of placing our own aging parents into senior living communities.

During visits, we kept hearing the same requests: favorite treats, a fun outing, and a dependable ride to appointments. Those moments showed us that what seniors often want most are not just services — they want experiences that feel personal, familiar, and cared for.

That is why B&B exists: to help communities offer residents wonderful experiences that feel like an extension of the family.

What makes B&B different

  • We built this from lived experience, not theory
  • We understand both the resident perspective and the family perspective
  • We focus on warmth, dignity, fun, and reliability
  • We make it easy for communities by handling the process end to end
