Our three experience pillars

Each pillar is designed to feel elevated, approachable, and easy for senior living communities to implement.

B&B Treats

A modern vending experience residents actually enjoy

B&B Treats brings intuitive, high-quality vending machines into senior living communities and turns them into something more personal, interactive, and thoughtfully curated.

  • Residents vote monthly on the treats and drinks they want to see stocked
  • Choices can include favorites like Milano Cookies, Cheez-It Crackers, trail mix, and hydration options like Pedialyte
  • We handle sourcing, stocking, restocking, and ongoing machine management
  • The result is a polished amenity that feels convenient, fun, and genuinely resident-centered

About B&B Experiences

We are a couple who built B&B Experiences after navigating the emotional and logistical realities of helping our own parents transition into senior living.

During visits, we kept hearing the same requests: favorite treats, a fun outing, and a reliable ride to appointments. Those moments made one thing clear: what seniors often want most are not just services, but experiences that feel personal, familiar, and cared for.

That is why B&B exists — to help communities offer wonderful experiences that feel like an extension of the family.

What makes B&B different

  • Built from lived experience, not theory
  • Created around both the resident perspective and the family perspective
  • Focused on warmth, dignity, fun, and reliability
  • Handled end to end so communities can offer more without adding more operational burden
